Our Story: 6 Years of Solar Excellence

Powering Kenya with sustainable energy solutions since 2019

Pioneering Solar Solutions in Kenya

Founded in 2019, PowerTeknica has been at the forefront of Kenya's renewable energy revolution. What started as a small team with a big vision has grown into one of the most trusted solar installation companies in the region.

Over the past 6 years, we've installed over 50 solar systems across residential, commercial, and agricultural sectors, helping our clients reduce their electricity costs by up to 90% while significantly lowering their carbon footprint.

Our journey began with a simple mission: to make clean, affordable solar energy accessible to every Kenyan household and business. Today, we're proud to have empowered thousands of Kenyans with energy independence while contributing to a greener, more sustainable future for our country.
