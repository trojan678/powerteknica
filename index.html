About Us: 9 Years of Solar Excellence

Powering Kenya with sustainable energy solutions since 2016

Pioneering Solar Solutions in Kenya

PowerTeknica Ltd is Specialized Power System Provider that was incorporated in 2016 with a mission to offer sustainable power systems solutions to clients from an installed capacity of 1.5kVA to 2000kVA.

Our client customer base comprises Domestic Installations, SMEs in various Fields as well as Financial Institutions and Real Estate Developers. We also undertake Solar Powered Water Projects.

We source most of our equipment directly from manufacturers in China as well as locally from such suppliers such as Chloride Exide and Ramco Group for smaller projects that require short installation lead times.
